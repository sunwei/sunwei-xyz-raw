MDFriday

I’m the creator of MDFriday, a tool that lets you turn Markdown files into websites — completely offline.
After almost three years of work, I rewrote the core of Hugo, one of the fastest static site generators in the world, so MDFriday works with 500+ Hugo themes.

Right now, we support Obsidian, which means you can turn your Markdown notes into multi-language websites with a single click — all on your local machine.
And this is just the start! We’ll be supporting more platforms soon, helping creators everywhere showcase their work easily.

With MDFriday, you can:

  • Build websites straight from your Markdown notes
  • Preview your site instantly with lightning-fast local updates
  • Choose from 500+ beautiful themes
  • Deploy your site in multiple ways
